Its about SUPPLY and DEMAND

The need for additional social housing is figured out by looking at how many people need housing (demand) and if current planned housing is capable of meeting that need (supply).

When there is not enough housing, families end up homeless and the poorly regulated private rented sector is relied on to meet need.
